Pricing

LW Alliance Helper is free to use at the base level for every alliance — every feature listed on the Commands page works on the free tier with sensible limits that fit a typical alliance. Premium lifts the limits and unlocks a handful of features built around member identity (DMs, mentions, roster sync).

Plans

PlanPriceBest for
Free$0 foreverMost alliances. Every feature works — just with sensible caps.
💎 Premium$4.99 / month
or $49 / year		Alliances that want member-aware automation: DMs, scheduled reminders, roster sync, multi-template, multi-survey.

Premium is billed by Discord through their App Subscriptions system — Discord handles all payments, taxes, refunds, and renewals. Run /upgrade in your leadership channel to open the subscription dialog. Premium activates automatically the moment a subscription becomes active. Cancellations through Discord deactivate Premium features at the end of the billing cycle; your saved data — including extra templates and surveys — is kept so you can resume any time.

If you'd like to support the bot without subscribing, run /donate for tip-jar links. 100% optional.

How to subscribe

  1. Make sure you have the leadership role configured during /setup.
  2. Run /upgrade in your leadership channel.
  3. Discord opens the subscription dialog. Confirm and pay.
  4. Premium activates immediately for the whole server. Run /setup_members next so the DM-based features can find your members.

Cancelling or pausing

Cancel at any time from User Settings → Authorized Apps or Settings → Subscriptions in Discord. Premium remains active until the end of the current billing cycle, then features lock. Your data — extra templates, multi-survey configs, scheduled reminders — is preserved so resubscribing later restores everything exactly as you left it.

If you cancel and resubscribe later, no data migration is needed.

Pricing FAQ

Do I need Premium to use the bot?

No. The free tier covers every feature with caps that fit a normal-sized alliance. Premium is for alliances that want member-aware automation (DMs, mentions, multi-survey, scheduled reminders).

Is there a per-member fee?

No. The price is per server, not per member. Add as many members to your alliance as you like.

Are there refunds?

Refunds are handled by Discord under their refund policy. The bot operator does not handle billing.

Can I try Premium before paying?

Discord doesn't expose a built-in trial system, but you can subscribe for one month, evaluate, and cancel — Discord prorates appropriately.
